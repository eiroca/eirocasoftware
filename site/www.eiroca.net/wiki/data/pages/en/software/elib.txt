====== eLib ======
Delphi library and components. Library for: Matrix, Matrix Math, Math & statistic, CSV, URL, Counters, dir/file scanning, file/string/... utils, progress bar, ...

Delphi Components.
^ Component  ^ Lang.               ^ Description ^
^DataList    | {{ico:eng.png|ENG}} |Set of data|
^eDB         | {{ico:eng.png|ENG}} |Extends TDatabase component to allow database identification, database messages notification, …|
^eGauge      | {{ico:eng.png|ENG}} |A 3 colors meter (usefull for progress indicators, x-meters, …)|
^eLineReport | {{ico:eng.png|ENG}} |Simple report engine line oriented. Supports preview, export to textfiles/HTML, …|
^eSillaITA   | {{ico:ita.png|ITA}} |Divide words into syllables (component or simple functions)|
